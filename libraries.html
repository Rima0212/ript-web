LIBRARY CAPACITY


The 300 sq m. Library & Reading room occupies a prominent position and it is an important and integral part of the teaching program of this Institute. It is not merely a depository of books, but an active workshop instrument in the production of or original thinking.
Currently Our Library has a good collection of about 7630 books and subscribes 12 journals & Periodicals. In order to access the digital platform freely, our library is also well equipped with computers & have good internet connection facilities.
