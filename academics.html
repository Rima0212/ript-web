PROGRAMMES


All courses are approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).
Courses running at present :


1. Three Year Full time Diploma in Printing Technology (Intake - 55)
2. Three Year Full time Diploma in Multimedia Technology (Intake - 60)
3. Three Year Full time Diploma in Photography (Intake - 40)

All the above courses are approved by AICTE and West Bengal State Council of Technical & Vocational Education & Skill Development (WBSCTVE&SD).

Course curriculum, syllabus, conducting examination and awarding diploma are given by WBSCTVE&SD.
